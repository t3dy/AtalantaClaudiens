Citrinitas PROCESS

The yellowing; intermediate stage of vivification between whiteness and redness.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier treats citrinitas as a transitional moment rather than a fully dramatized stage, and no single emblem is devoted to it exclusively. De Jong notes that Tilton reads the yellowing into the geometric progression of Emblem XXI, where the soul-as-air phase corresponds to the intermediate vivification between albedo and rubedo.
