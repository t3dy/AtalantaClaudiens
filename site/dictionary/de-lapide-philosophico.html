De Lapide Philosophico SOURCE_TEXT

Alchemical emblem book by Lambsprinck pairing symbolic engravings with verse explanations, a key prototype for the visual emblem tradition Maier inherits.

In Atalanta Fugiens

De Jong and Pagel identify Lambsprinck's emblem book as a prototype for Maier's method of encoding alchemical doctrine in pictorial form, with Emblem VII's winged and wingless bird imagery drawing on a shared symbolic vocabulary of volatile and fixed principles.
