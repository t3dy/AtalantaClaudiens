Fama Fraternitatis SOURCE_TEXT

First Rosicrucian manifesto (1614), announcing the Brotherhood of the Rose Cross and calling for a universal reformation of knowledge through alchemy and spiritual illumination.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Tilton argues that Maier's Atalanta Fugiens responds directly to the Rosicrucian moment inaugurated by the Fama, and that Maier actively sought contact with the Brotherhood. While no emblem motto derives directly from the Fama, the manifesto provides the ideological framework within which Maier's synthesis becomes intelligible.

Source: de_jong_1969, tilton_2003
