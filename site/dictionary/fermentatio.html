Fermentatio PROCESS

Fermentation; the penultimate operation that introduces the ferment completing the Stone's transformation.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier treats fermentation as the vivifying operation that brings the nearly completed Stone to life, analogous to the leavening of bread or the action of a seed in prepared earth. De Jong connects this to the agricultural metaphors of Emblem VI, where the ferment of gold activates the receptive white earth to produce the final multiplication.
