Hippomenes FIGURE

The pursuer in the frontispiece race; represents fixed Sulphur pursuing volatile Mercury with golden apples as catalysts.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier casts Hippomenes as the alchemical adept who must use strategy and the golden apples of Art to slow and fix the fleeing volatile principle. De Jong reads the golden apples as the catalytic agents of the Work, without which brute pursuit of Mercury would be futile, making Hippomenes a figure for the disciplined application of alchemical knowledge.
