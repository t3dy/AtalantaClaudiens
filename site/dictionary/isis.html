Isis FIGURE

Egyptian goddess who reassembles the dismembered Osiris; represents the lunar anima that restores the dissolved solar principle to wholeness.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier presents Isis in Emblem XLIV as the feminine restorative principle who gathers the scattered parts of the slain Osiris, encoding the reintegration phase of the alchemical Work. De Jong connects this to the broader pattern of female figures in AF who preserve and reconstitute what the masculine principle alone cannot.
