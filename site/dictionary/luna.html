Luna SUBSTANCE

Silver; the lunar principle; the feminine receptive agent in alchemical transmutation.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier figures Luna as the volatile, feminine counterpart to Sol, embodied in the fleeing Atalanta of the frontispiece and the winged figures of Emblems VII and XVI. De Jong traces Maier's lunar symbolism to the Senior Zadith tradition, where Moon and Sun must unite to produce the perfected Stone.
