Mortificatio PROCESS

The killing or death of the old form; the destruction of existing structure as prerequisite for renewal. Closely related to putrefaction and nigredo.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier dramatizes mortificatio through the death scenes that pervade the second half of Atalanta Fugiens, including the death of Osiris (XLIV) and the mutual destruction of dragon and woman (L). De Jong reads these deaths as encoding the necessary destruction of the impure form before the perfected Stone can emerge.
