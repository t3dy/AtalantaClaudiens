Opus Magnum CONCEPT

The Great Work; the entire alchemical project of transmutation from base matter to the perfected Stone.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier conceives Atalanta Fugiens itself as a guide to the Opus Magnum, with the fifty-emblem sequence mapping the stages of the Work from cosmological foundation through purification, conjunction, and final perfection. De Jong argues that the deliberate ordering of emblems reflects the sequential logic of the Great Work, making the book a symbolic enactment of the alchemical process it describes.
