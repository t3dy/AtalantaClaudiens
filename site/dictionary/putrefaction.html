Putrefaction PROCESS

Putrefactio
Controlled decomposition; the death of old form that enables new creation. Associated with nigredo.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier dramatizes putrefaction through the suffering of King Duenech in Emblem XXVIII and the death of Osiris in Emblem XLIV, both representing the necessary death of the old form before resurrection. De Jong and Wescott both identify the four-emblem King sequence (XXIV, XXVIII, XXXI, XLIV) as Maier's extended meditation on controlled decomposition as the gateway to renewal.
