Regina FIGURE

The Queen figure representing Luna, silver, or the feminine volatile principle in alchemical allegory.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier pairs the Queen with the King as the feminine lunar counterpart necessary for the coniunctio, appearing in the marriage and union emblems where male and female principles must join. De Jong reads the Queen as Maier's figure for the receptive mercurial principle that completes the King's solar fixity.
