Rex FIGURE

The King figure representing Sol, gold, or the masculine fixed principle in alchemical allegory.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier deploys the King figure across a sustained four-emblem sequence (XXIV, XXVIII, XXXI, XLVIII) that De Jong and Wescott identify as the Alchemical King cycle, dramatizing the sickness, death, and resurrection of the solar principle. The King's suffering and cure encode the entire Work in miniature, from nigredo through rubedo.
