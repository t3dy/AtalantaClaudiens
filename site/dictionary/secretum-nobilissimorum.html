Secretum Nobilissimorum SOURCE_TEXT

Sixteenth-century alchemical text by Jodocus Greverus containing allegorical readings of classical myths as encoded alchemical teachings.

In Atalanta Fugiens

De Jong identifies Greverus alongside Robertus Vallensis as a source for the frontispiece commentary of Atalanta Fugiens. Together these authorities establish the precedent for reading the Atalanta-Hippomenes myth as an allegory of the volatile-fixed dynamic in the Great Work.
