Tabula Chimica SOURCE_TEXT

Latin translation of an alchemical work by Muhammad ibn Umail (Senior Zadith), containing visionary imagery of volatile and fixed principles as paired birds.

In Atalanta Fugiens

De Jong traces the winged and wingless bird motif of Emblem VII directly to Senior Zadith's Tabula Chimica, where the two birds represent the volatile Mercury and fixed Sulphur that must be bonded. This source establishes the bird symbolism central to Maier's depiction of the volatile-fixed dynamic.
