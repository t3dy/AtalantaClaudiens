Turba Philosophorum SOURCE_TEXT

Assembly of Philosophers; major alchemical compilation of Arabic origin. Source for 9 AF mottos per De Jong.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier draws more motto material from the Turba Philosophorum than from any other single source, with De Jong attributing nine emblem mottos to this Arabic-Latin compilation. The Turba's influence is particularly prominent in the washing emblems (III), the hermaphrodite sequence (XXXIII), and the final emblem (L), where Theophilus's teaching on mutual destruction provides the culminating image.
