Vas Hermeticum CONCEPT

The hermetically sealed vessel; the closed system in which all alchemical transformation occurs.

In Atalanta Fugiens

Maier figures the sealed vessel through the philosophical egg of Emblem VIII and the enclosed spaces where transformations occur throughout the sequence. De Jong identifies the vas hermeticum as the operational prerequisite for the entire Work, since only a perfectly sealed system can prevent the loss of volatile spirits during heating and dissolution.
