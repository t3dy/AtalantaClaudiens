Overview

Emblem I bears the motto: "His nurseis the earth."

Maier's Source Texts

De Jong identifies the motto source as Tabula Smaragdina (Emerald Tablet). The discourse draws on Rosarium Philosophorum.

Alchemical Significance

H.M.E. de Jong notes: Motto derives from Tabula Smaragdina. Food becomes substance of the nourished. Alchemical work mirrors nature's processes.

Related Terms

Opus Magnum, Prima Materia, Tabula Smaragdina
