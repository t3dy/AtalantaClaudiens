Emblem II — Its nurse is the Earth

Assembled from De Jong (1969) corpus extraction. Not reviewed by a human scholar. Citations should be verified against original sources.

Overview

Emblem II bears the motto: "Its nurse is the Earth"

Maier's Source Texts

De Jong identifies the motto source as Tabula Smaragdina (Emerald Tablet).

Alchemical Significance

Walter Pagel notes: Notes the pregnant female with terrestrial globe as 'nurse of tender child.'

H.M.E. de Jong notes: Retort (artificial) and nature work together. Art serves Nature as teacher.
