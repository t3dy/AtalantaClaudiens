Scholarly Commentary

H.M.E. de Jong HIGH

Brother (hot, dry, choleric) = Fire. Sister (cold, moist, phlegmatic) = Water. Union of contrasts per Empedocles. Related to Emblems XXXIX (Oedipus/mother), XLI (Myrrha/father), XLIV (Osiris/Isis).
