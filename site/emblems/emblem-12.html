Epigram
Everyoneknows Latona’stwins, Who, accordingtothelegend, were children of Jupiter. Othersassert thatthetwins werea unionof the sunlight And the moon, which has black stainson itsface. Thereforeget yourselfready, to make Latona white. And without delaytear up the unclearbooks, whichharmyou.
