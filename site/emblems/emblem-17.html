Overview

Emblem XVII bears the motto: "Thefire likesmaking thingsfiery, but not, like thegold, making gold."

Maier's Source Texts

The discourse draws on Senior Zadith (Muhammad ibn Umail), Tabula Chimica.

Alchemical Significance

H.M.E. de Jong notes: Four kinds of fire: common fire (Vulcan), fire contrary to nature (Mercury/dragon), fire against nature, and natural fire. Derived from Lullius and the Turba tradition.
