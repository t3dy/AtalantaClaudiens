What You See

Wisdom-centered emblem at the book's midpoint. Dense scriptural marginalia. Sapientia as personification of alchemical knowledge joined with practice.

Alchemical Significance

H.M.E. de Jong: Central emblem of the book. Motto from Proverbs 3:18 but through Rosarium, not arbitrary biblical selection. Unusually dense scriptural marginalia. Maier foregrounds religious-sapiential basis of entire work. Links to Emblem XXVII.
