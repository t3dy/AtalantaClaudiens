XLI

Epigram
From her ownfat her, Myrrha received thebeautiful Adonis, Thefavourite of Venus, whomaboar killed. Venus rushedup, and, wounding herfoot, Sheherselfcoloured red with her blood the rose, whichhadbeen whiteatfirst. The Goddessweeps—the Syrians weep, and the wholeworldis plunged into deepmourning And sheput Adonisdown under the tenderlettuce.
