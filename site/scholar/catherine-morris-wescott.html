Catherine Morris Wescott

Music and Alchemy

Modal analysis of Maier's fugues as text-painting for alchemical Putrefaction and Albification

Musicologist who analyzed four 'Alchemical King' emblems (XXIV, XXVIII, XXXI, XLIV) in Atalanta Fugiens, demonstrating how Maier used modal shifts and chromaticism in the fugues to parallel the King's transformation through Putrefaction and Albification.
