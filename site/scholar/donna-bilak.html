Donna Bilak

Art history, steganography

Co-editor of Furnace and Fugue; Maier and steganography

Donna Bilak is a material culture scholar and art historian who co-edited Furnace and Fugue (2020), the first born-digital scholarly edition of the Atalanta Fugiens. Her research emphasizes Maier's use of steganographic techniques -- the embedding of hidden messages within the multimedia structure of the emblem book -- and approaches the work through the lens of making, craft, and material practice. Bilak's digital edition, built on open-access principles, represents a significant methodological departure from De Jong's print-based source criticism, offering interactive tools for exploring the relationships among image, text, and music that Maier designed as a unified sensory experience.
