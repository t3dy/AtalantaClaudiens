Tara Nummedal

History of science, digital humanities

Co-editor of Furnace and Fugue digital edition

Tara Nummedal is a historian of science who co-edited Furnace and Fugue (2020) alongside Donna Bilak, bringing expertise in the social and institutional history of early modern alchemy to the digital edition of the Atalanta Fugiens. Her scholarship situates Maier's work within the broader landscape of alchemical practice, patronage, and knowledge circulation in the Holy Roman Empire. As co-editor, Nummedal helped frame the Furnace and Fugue project as a digital humanities experiment, extending the interpretive tradition anchored by De Jong's monograph into new modes of interactive, multimedia scholarly presentation.
