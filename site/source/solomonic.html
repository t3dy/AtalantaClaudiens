Solomonic / Biblical Wisdom Tradition (Proverbs) BIBLICAL

cited in 14 emblems

The Solomonic wisdom tradition, centered on the Book of Proverbs and its personification of Wisdom as a tree of life, provides the religious-philosophical foundation for the sapiential dimension of Atalanta Fugiens. De Jong (1969) demonstrates that the motto of Emblem XXVI derives from Proverbs 3:18, though Maier receives this biblical text not through direct exegesis but through the mediating tradition of the Rosarium Philosophorum. The unusually dense scriptural marginalia of Emblems XXVI and XXVII signals that Maier intends these central emblems as a theological grounding for the entire alchemical enterprise.
