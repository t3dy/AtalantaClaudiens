Stage of the Great Work

Albedo

The Whitening

"Make Latona white and tear up the books." — Atalanta Fugiens, motto of Emblem XI

The second stage: purification by washing, sublimation, and distillation. The Moon presides; phlegmatic constitution restored; matter purified to lunar silver. In Atalanta Fugiens this is the washerwoman of Emblem III, the whitening of Latona (XI–XII), the white-lead cooking of Emblem XXII, the sowing of gold in white earth (VI).
