Stage of the Great Work

Nigredo

The Blackening

"Putrefaction is the death of bodies and the cause of generation." — pseudo-Geber, Summa Perfectionis

The first stage of the Great Work: putrefaction, blackening, and the death of the prima materia. Saturn presides; black bile and melancholy mark the body. The matter must die before it can be reborn. In Atalanta Fugiens this is the work of dragons devouring their tails (XIV), the king drowning (XXXI), the dragon-and-woman mortal embrace (L), the slaying of Osiris (XLIV).
